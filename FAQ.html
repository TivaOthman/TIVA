If you get seasick, there are a few things you can do to help alleviate the symptoms. Consider choosing a cabin in the middle of the ship and on a lower deck, as these areas tend to have less motion. Consult with a healthcare professional and consider taking over-the-counter or prescription medication for seasickness. Stay hydrated, eat light meals, and focus on the horizon or a fixed point in the distance to reduce the sensation of motion. Additionally, fresh air on deck and natural remedies like ginger can provide relief.
